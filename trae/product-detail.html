Product Description

Midnight Bloom is the epitome of luxury fragrance, crafted by our master perfumer Alexandre Dubois. This exquisite eau de parfum is a celebration of nocturnal florals, capturing the enchanting scent of flowers that bloom under the moonlight.

The fragrance opens with a sparkling blend of bergamot and black currant, creating a fresh and vibrant introduction. The heart notes reveal a sophisticated bouquet of jasmine, violet, and night-blooming orchid, evoking the mysterious allure of a moonlit garden. As the fragrance settles, the base notes of amber, musk, and vanilla emerge, leaving a warm, sensual trail that lingers on the skin.

Each bottle of Midnight Bloom is meticulously crafted and filled by hand in our Paris atelier, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail. The elegant glass bottle, adorned with a hand-tied silk ribbon, is a testament to our commitment to luxury and craftsmanship.

Key Notes

  • Top Notes: Bergamot, Black Currant
  • Heart Notes: Jasmine, Violet, Night-blooming Orchid
  • Base Notes: Amber, Musk, Vanilla
